ABOUT ME
Hello! My name is Jay and I am currently a freshman studying Data Science at UC Berkeley. This project idea came from a personal struggle of mine. When I'm throwing something away, I try to throw it in the correct bin, but often times I don't know which bin it belongs in and end up standing awkwardly in front of the trash bins for a few seconds. For anyone who experiences the same thing, I hope this website helps!
